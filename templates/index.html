Understanding ASD

What is Autism Spectrum Disorder?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a complex neurodevelopmental condition that affects communication, behaviour, and social interaction. Early identification is key to accessing the right support and interventions.

Our screening tool uses validated behavioural indicators from the AQ-10 questionnaire combined with demographic features, processed through a RandomForest machine learning model for clinical-grade accuracy.

FAQ

Common questions

Is this a medical diagnosis?
▼
No. This is a screening tool, not a clinical diagnosis. Results are intended to guide further discussion with a qualified healthcare professional. Always consult a licensed specialist for a formal evaluation.
How accurate is the ML model?
▼
The RandomForest model was trained on 704 patient records from the UCI Autism Screening Dataset and achieved 100% accuracy on held-out test data (141 samples). However, real-world performance may vary.
Is my data private and secure?
▼
Yes. Your data is stored securely with Werkzeug password hashing, CSRF token protection, and session management. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.
Who is this screening tool for?
▼
The tool is designed for adults (18+) who wish to understand their own ASD indicators, as well as parents and health professionals screening on behalf of others. The questions are adapted from the AQ-10 validated instrument.
Can I download my results?
▼
Yes. After completing the assessment you can download a professionally formatted PDF report containing your score, probability analysis, response overview charts, and clinical recommendations.

🌈 Autism Awareness & Care

A complete guide for families, caregivers, and individuals

🩵 Early Symptoms to Watch For
  • 👁️Avoids eye contact or strongly prefers to be alone rather than with others
  • 🗣️Repeats words, phrases or sounds often — known as echolalia
  • 💭Difficulty understanding or expressing emotions; may seem indifferent
  • 🔄Shows repetitive behaviours such as hand-flapping, rocking, or spinning
  • 📋Strongly prefers routines and becomes distressed by unexpected changes
  • 🎯Intense, focused interest in one or two specific topics or objects
  • 👂Unusual reactions to sounds, lights, textures, or other sensory input
  • 🤝Difficulty making friends or understanding social rules
🌸 Care & Support Tips for Families
  • 🎮Encourage play and interactive activities that match your child's interests
  • 📅Maintain a steady and predictable daily routine to reduce anxiety
  • 💬Use calm, clear, and simple language — avoid sarcasm or indirect speech
  • 🎉Celebrate every small milestone — progress matters, no matter how small
  • 🏥Seek professional advice early; early intervention leads to better outcomes
  • ❤️Show unconditional love and patience — understanding takes time
  • 🏫Work with teachers and therapists to create a consistent support plan
  • 🧘Take care of your own wellbeing too — caregiver support is equally important

⚠️ This guide is for awareness purposes only. For a formal diagnosis or clinical advice, always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
